Our Mission

To provide a world class travel experiance for International Students visiting New York

The New York Experiance

New York is a city that has many world class attractions. In addition to these attractions there are thousands of local venues where the true essence of New York can be found. International Students come to the NYC area to experience something that culture; an experience that cannot be found anywhere else in the world.

International Students often do not have the time or the resources to visit all the great locations of the city. That is why our team of international students decided to make this travel website. We offer hand-picked locations that cater to the needs of international students.
